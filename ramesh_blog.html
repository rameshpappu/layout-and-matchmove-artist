About Me

I am a seasoned Layout and Matchmove Artist with over a decade of experience in the visual effects industry, currently leveraging my expertise at DNEG—one of the world’s premier visual effects studios. I specialize in creating realistic and immersive scenes for feature films and television, blending technical precision with artistic innovation.

Core Strengths:

  • Seamlessly integrating CG elements into live-action footage.
  • Designing and positioning digital assets to craft compelling virtual environments.
  • Solving complex spatial challenges to ensure continuity and alignment with the director’s vision.

Proficient in industry-leading tools such as Maya, 3DEqualizer, and Nuke, I excel at transforming creative ideas into visually stunning results. Collaboration is at the heart of my work; I thrive in dynamic team environments, working closely with artists, technicians, and supervisors to deliver high-quality outputs that captivate audiences.

Driven by an unwavering passion for visual storytelling, I am committed to pushing the boundaries of visual effects to exceed expectations and inspire audiences worldwide.
